### About DLSS Enabler Manager

**DLSS Enabler Manager** is a powerful, user-friendly utility developed by **Nodix Tech**, designed specifically as the ultimate companion for the **DLSS Enabler** mod. Our tool is dedicated to helping PC gamers effortlessly manage, install, and update the DLSS Enabler along with Frame Generation and Streamline components. 

Whether you are looking to unlock DLSS 3 Frame Generation on older graphics cards or simply maximize your game's performance, the manager automates the complex file handling. It ensures a safe, stable, and massive FPS boost without the headache of manual modding.

### Key Features

* 🎯 **Dedicated DLSS Enabler Support:** Built from the ground up to seamlessly integrate with the DLSS Enabler mod, automatically handling all specific prerequisites, custom DLLs, and streamline files.
* 🔍 **Smart Game Scanner:** Automatically detects your installed games across all drives, natively supporting libraries from Steam, Epic Games, and even manual game directories.
* ✅ **Intelligent Compatibility Check:** Deeply analyzes game folders to verify Streamline versions (2.4+) and required files, ensuring the DLSS Enabler will work flawlessly before you hit install.
* 🛡️ **Safety First (Auto-Backup):** Never worry about breaking your games. The manager automatically creates secure backups of your original game files prior to applying any mods.
* ↩️ **One-Click Rollback:** Experiencing issues or want to play multiplayer? Instantly uninstall the mod and restore your game to its pristine, original state with a single click.
* 🤖 **Built-in AI Assistant:** Features an integrated, context-aware AI technical expert ready to guide you, answer your modding questions, and provide troubleshooting advice tailored to your hardware.
* 🐧 **Cross-Platform Readiness:** Designed with stability in mind, featuring robust compatibility measures to run smoothly on Windows, as well as Linux and Steam Deck (via Proton/Wine).

### How to use:

1. Launch the application and scan for your installed games.
2. Check the DLSS Enabler compatibility status for your desired game.
3. Click **"Install Mod"** and enjoy your massive FPS boost!
